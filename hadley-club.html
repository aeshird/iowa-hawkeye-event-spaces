Hadley Club Room

The Hadley Club Room is located inside of Carver Hawkeye Arena. This 1,200 square foot space is an intimate space ideal for luncheons, showers, birthday parties, small meetings and social other gatherings.

Security

Each individual event is subject to require security personnel in addition to the rental pricing. Once we have reviewed the specific event in detail, a decision will be made on whether or not security will be required.

Handicap Accessibility

There is guest access to the ground level entrance of the building as well as an elevator inside for access to each event space.

Parking

Parking is available Monday-Friday in Lot 46B (Carver Parking Lot) starting at 4:30pm and all day on weekends. Parking is available weekdays at an hourly rate in the Dental Lot located South of the main entrance to Carver. Guests who park in lot 46B before 4:30pm on weekdays are subject to non-refundable fees.
