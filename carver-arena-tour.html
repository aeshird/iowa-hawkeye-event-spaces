Carver-Hawkeye Arena

Guided tours of Carver-Hawkeye Arena are available for a fee. The tour is approximately 60 minutes and features access to restricted areas such as the team locker room, press row, and premium seating.

Cost

$20 per adult / $10 per child

* Please note that children over the age of 13 are not allowed on tours due to compliance.

Features

Arena Floor, Home Team Locker Rooms, Press Row, Terrace Tables

* Please note that tour locations may vary due weather, previously scheduled events, and other unforeseen circumstances.

Handicap Accessibility

We can accommodate your request but your tour will be limited. Please make sure that you note this on the tour request form. We encourage that you provide your own wheelchair/walker/cane.

Parking

Parking is available weekdays at an hourly rate at Parking Ramp 3 behind Kinnick Stadium. Guests who park in Lot 46 before 4:30 PM on weekdays are subject to non-refundable fees.

Payment

Credit card payments only. No cash payments will be accepted.

Discounted rates are available for approved non-profits, current UIowa Students, Active Military Members and University of Iowa Departments.

Once tickets are purchased, they are non-refundable. We will be happy to reschedule your reservation at a later date.

Prohibited Items

Food/Drink

Pets -- with the exception of Service Animals (please let us know ahead of time so we are aware)

Request a Tour

Please fill out our contact form or email
ainsley-shird@uiowa.edu to set-up a tour.

* The tour will begin in the North Lobby. Please be on time for your tour (10 minutes early). If you do not arrive on time, the tour will start without you. (Tour tickets are non-refundable.)

Rules & Regulations

Tours MUST be scheduled at least two weeks in advance.
Tours subject to tour guide's availability and are NOT available on weekends/holidays.
No tours will be permitted on and/or the day before a home basketball game, wrestling, or gymnastics meet.
No professional pictures are allowed on tours.
Absolutely no food or drink is permitted in Carver-Hawkeye Arena.
No footballs allowed.

Tour Limitations

Limits are set depending on staffing, the time of year, the group, etc. This can be discussed during the reservation process. However, there is a minimum of four people required for a tour to happen.

Weather

If it's going to rain, snow, be too hot/cold, etc. on the day of the tour, you can reschedule your tour for a later date no later than 48 hours to your tour date. However, please note that during busy times, the next available tour may be 4 weeks or more in the future.
