McCord Club Lounge

The McCord Club Lounge is a climate-controlled space that includes the Indoor Club stadium seating. The east side of the building includes a wall of windows that allows for a spectacular view of The Iowa Hawkeye Football playing field and The University of Iowa Hospitals and Clinics. The west side of the building includes a wall of windows that provides a view of the Hansen Football Performance Center. This 3,200 square foot space is equipped with a drop-down LCD projector and screen making this space great for client presentations and employee meetings. This space is also ideal for banquets, receptions, holiday parties, wedding ceremonies, and other social gatherings.
