Carver-Hawkeye Arena

Welcome to Carver Hawkeye Arena Premium Event Space. Named for the late Roy Carver, a long-time supporter of the University of Iowa, Carver-Hawkeye Arena is one of the 25 largest University-owned facilities in the nation. The grand opening took place in January of 1983 and in September of 2011 it underwent a multi-million dollar renovation. With the renovation came two indoor event spaces. We invite you to take a look around and explore the Feller Club Room, and the Hadley Club Room.

Each space is available to The University and to the public for events and gatherings. Located on campus, just two miles off of Interstate 80 it is easily accessible from any direction.
