Athletic Director's Suite

The University of Iowa Athletic Director's Suite is perched high above Kinnick Stadium. This 425 square foot suite boasts the best view of the UI campus, along with a view of The Iowa Hawkeye playing field and The University of Iowa Hospitals and Clinics. This intimate space is excellent for business meetings, luncheons, showers, birthday parties, and other social gatherings.
